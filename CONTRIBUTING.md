# Contributing

Thank you for contributing to DeltaModel Architecture Assistant.

## Licensing of Contributions

- By submitting a contribution, you agree that your contribution is licensed under `AGPL-3.0-only`.
- Ensure you have the right to contribute the code and any included assets.
- Do not submit proprietary or third-party content that cannot be distributed under AGPL.

## Developer Certificate of Origin (DCO)

By contributing, you certify that:

- The contribution was created by you, or
- You have the right to submit it under the project license.

Use a signed-off commit message line:

`Signed-off-by: Your Name <your.email@example.com>`

## Practical Notes

- Keep changes focused and well-scoped.
- Include tests or validation steps where possible.
- Document user-visible behavior changes in `README.md` when relevant.
